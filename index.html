WorkOS

How TreasuryPath's AI skill system turns a general-purpose AI into a team of domain experts. No database. No API. Just markdown playbooks, JSON files, and a really good model.

The idea: When you say /cfo, the AI loads a 1,100-line playbook that teaches it finance frameworks, gives it a persona, tells it where the data lives, and defines exactly what to output. Same AI, totally different expertise depending on which playbook it reads. Like handing a consultant a different briefing book before each meeting.

This page walks through the CFO skill as a concrete example. The same pattern powers all 61 skills across CEO, CMO, CPO, CTO, CISO, GTM, coaching, travel, and more.
